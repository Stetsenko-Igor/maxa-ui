# Social Button

## Purpose

Social Button provides provider sign-in and account-linking actions.

## Anatomy

- Color provider icon.
- Provider label.
- White outline-button surface with 8px radius, border, and `Shadows/sm`.

## Variants

- Primary Figma Social Buttons set: `google`, `linkedin`.
- Additional provider presets: `apple`, `facebook`, `github`, `instagram`, `pinterest`, `reddit`, `telegram`, `tiktok`, `twitter`, `whatsapp`, `x`, `youtube`.
- Optional custom icon.

## States

- Default: white outline-button surface, gray border, `Shadows/sm`.
- Hover: white surface, stronger border/shadow.
- Focus: white surface with the Figma light-blue focus border treatment.

## Accessibility

Decorative provider icons are hidden from the accessible name; the visible label names the action.
